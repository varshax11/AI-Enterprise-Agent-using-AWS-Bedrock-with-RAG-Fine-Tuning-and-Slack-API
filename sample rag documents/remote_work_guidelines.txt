# Remote Work Guidelines

1. **Purpose**  
   To define expectations and procedures for employees working remotely.

2. **Scope**  
   Applies to all employees approved for remote work arrangements.

3. **Guidelines**  
   a. Employees must use company-provided devices with updated security patches.  
   b. VPN connection is required when accessing company systems.  
   c. Confidential information must not be shared on personal devices or public networks.  
   d. Daily check-ins with managers are required via Teams or Slack.

4. **Communication**  
   a. Use corporate email for all business communications.  
   b. Report any IT issues immediately to the IT helpdesk.  

5. **Compliance**  
   Employees must adhere to all company policies, including ISO27001 security requirements and NDA agreements
